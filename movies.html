My Favorite Shows and Movies


As I mentioned before, I'm a huge fan of horror, sci-fi and mystery shows and movies.

For streaming shows, I really enjoyed

Squid Game, Vikings: Valhalla and Wednesday on Netflix.

I also enjoyed Picard, Star Trek Discovery, and

Star Trek Prodigy (which was really cute in a Trekkie nerd kind of way) from Paramount+ .

For movies, I loved both versions of Stephen King's movie IT,

as well as international horror movies, like Ju-On: The Grudge.
